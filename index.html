About Me

I am an ESL teacher currently looking to become a web developer. I've dabbled with scripting for a computer game in the past, so I know that I enjoy both the creativity and the logic that goes into coding, as well as the ecstatic "eureka" moments that come when I figure out how to perform a particularly daunting task.

Despite that, however, I would still very much characterize myself as a beginner, both because the only coding I've done has been relatively basic, and because it's been more than a year now since I was working on scripts for the aforementioned game. Still, the memory of that experience and the prospect of a career change have me very excited to learn how to code!

In my free time, I enjoy gaming, reading, cycling and hiking, as well as many other things.
